Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 June 2026

Polardex is a personal Pokémon TCG collection tracker. This policy explains what information the app handles and how. We keep it minimal on purpose: Polardex is built for tracking cards, not for collecting data about you.

Information we handle

Account. When you sign in, we store a username and password to authenticate you (via Google Firebase Authentication). We do not collect your real name, email address, or phone number.

Your collection. The cards you track — quantities, variants, and any notes you add — are stored in our database (Google Firebase) so they sync across your devices.

Camera. The scan feature uses your camera to identify cards. Image recognition runs entirely on your device; photos are never stored or uploaded. Only the recognised text (a card's name and number) is sent to the public Pokémon TCG API to look up the matching card.

Face ID / Touch ID. Biometric unlock is handled by iOS. Polardex never sees your biometric data. If you enable quick unlock, your sign-in credentials are stored securely in the device Keychain and never leave your device.

What we don't do

  • No advertising and no ad tracking.
  • No analytics or third-party tracking SDKs.
  • No location, contacts, or microphone access.
  • We never sell or share your information.

Third-party services

Polardex uses a small number of services to function:

  • Google Firebase — authentication and database.
  • Pokémon TCG API — card data and artwork.
  • PokeAPI / PokémonDB — Pokémon images.
  • open.er-api.com — currency exchange rates (no personal data sent).

These services receive only what's needed to return card and image data; they do not receive your account credentials.

Data retention & deletion

You can remove cards from your collection at any time within the app. To delete your account and associated data, contact us using the address below and we'll remove it.

Children

Polardex is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from them.
